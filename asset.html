Asset

Introduction

An asset is a resource that an individual, corporation, or country has control over and holds with the expectation of deriving a future benefit from it. Assets are recorded on a company's balance sheet and can be classified as current, fixed, financial, or intangible. Companies acquire or develop assets to enhance their value or operations. Assets can be anything that has the potential to generate cash flow, lower expenses, or increase sales in the future, whether it's manufacturing equipment or a patent.
