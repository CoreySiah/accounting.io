Taxation

Introduction

Governments, whether local, regional, or national, require individuals or corporations to pay taxes as compulsory contributions. Tax revenues support various government activities, including public works and services such as roads and schools, as well as programs like Social Security and Medicare. In economics, taxes impose a burden on whoever pays them, whether it's the taxed entity, such as a business, or the end consumers of the business's products. There are various taxes to consider from an accounting perspective, such as payroll taxes, federal and state income taxes, and sales taxes.
